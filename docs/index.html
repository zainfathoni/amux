Local lifecycle for Amp

Workers. Runners. One workspace.

amux makes local Amp clients restorable and inspectable. Canonical selectors, dry-run plans, JSON v1, and fail-closed verification make its lifecycle safe to hand to agents.

$ amux --dry-run launch --workspace product
$ amux launch --workspace product
$ amux doctor --workspace product

Worker

Interactive, thread-bound Amp client. Canonical identity: --thread.

Runner

Non-interactive amp --no-tui client. Canonical identity: --workdir.

Workspace

A lifecycle group for both modes, represented by one same-named tmux session.

Bundled agent skill

Say what outcome you want.

The /amux skill routes “Pin it”, “Park it”, “Shelve this”, health, durable issue-worker coordination, sprawl, and finish to the smallest safe side effect. Reports and group history are durable; tmux callbacks only wake a verified coordinator. Health, coordination, sprawl, and finish remain skill-only workflows.
